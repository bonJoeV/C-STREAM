## Lesson Procedure

### Opening Circle (6 minutes)
1. **Ecosystem Definition:**
   - "A community of living things interacting with their environment"
   - All living + non-living things work together
2. **Key Vocabulary:**
   - **Producer** ‚Äî Makes own food (plants)
   - **Consumer** ‚Äî Eats others for energy
   - **Decomposer** ‚Äî Breaks down dead things
3. **Food Chain vs Web:**
   - Chain: Single path of energy (grass ‚Üí rabbit ‚Üí fox)
   - Web: Multiple connected chains (more realistic!)
4. **St. Kateri** ‚Äî Saw God's design in nature

### Main Activity: Food Web Exploration (26 minutes)

**Part 1: Food Chain Building (6 minutes)**

- Start simple: sun ‚Üí producer ‚Üí consumer ‚Üí decomposer

- Minnesota examples:
  - Sun ‚Üí grass ‚Üí deer ‚Üí wolf ‚Üí bacteria
  - Sun ‚Üí algae ‚Üí minnow ‚Üí bass ‚Üí eagle

- Energy flows from sun through all living things!

**Part 2: Food Web Game (10 minutes)**

- Students receive organism cards (plants, animals, decomposers)

- Stand in circle

- Pass yarn to show connections:
  - "I am a plant, I'm eaten by..." ‚Üí pass yarn
  - Continue building web

- Everyone holds yarn‚Äîsee the connections!

- What happens if one organism is removed? (Pull yarn‚Äîsee effects!)

**Part 3: Create Food Web Poster (8 minutes)**

- Choose ecosystem: forest, prairie, wetland, pond

- Draw food web showing:
  - At least 3 producers
  - At least 4 consumers
  - At least 2 decomposers
  - Arrows showing energy flow

- Label: Producer, Consumer, Decomposer

**Part 4: Share & Discuss (2 minutes)**

- Show webs

- "What happens if _____ disappears?"

- Everything is connected!

### Engineering Journal (5 minutes)
1. Draw a simple food chain (4 organisms)
2. Label: Producer, Consumer, Decomposer
3. Write: "Everything is connected because..."
4. Write: "We should protect ecosystems because..."

### Closing Circle (3 minutes)
1. **Connection Appreciation** ‚Äî "What surprised you about food webs?"
2. **Stewardship** ‚Äî "How can we protect ecosystems?"
3. **Closing Prayer** ‚Äî *"God, your creation is beautifully connected. Help us protect the web of life you designed. Amen."*
