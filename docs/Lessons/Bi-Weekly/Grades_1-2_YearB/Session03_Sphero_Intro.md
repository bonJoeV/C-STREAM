## Lesson Procedure

### Opening Circle (5 minutes)
1. **Meet Sphero!** ‚Äî Show the robot
   - "This is Sphero‚Äîa robot ball!"
   - "WE tell it what to do!"
2. **Robot Review:**
   - Robots follow instructions
   - They don't think‚Äîthey obey our commands
3. **Blessed Carlo** ‚Äî Used computers to serve God
4. **Today's Goal** ‚Äî "Learn to control Sphero!"

### Main Activity: Sphero Introduction (19 minutes)

**Part 1: First Connection (4 minutes)**

- Small groups receive Sphero and tablet

- Open Sphero Edu app

- Connect to Sphero (teacher helps)

- Explore: Touch the screen‚Äîwhat happens?

**Part 2: Basic Driving (7 minutes)**

- Use "Drive" mode

- Practice:
  - Go forward
  - Go backward
  - Turn left/right
  - Change speed (slow is better for control!)

- Challenge: Drive in a straight line

**Part 3: Path Challenge (6 minutes)**

- Simple taped path on floor

- Challenge: Drive Sphero along the path

- Stay on the line!

- Partners take turns

**Part 4: Color Fun (2 minutes)**

- Change Sphero's colors

- Make it flash!

- "Robots can do fun things too!"

### Design Detective Journal (4 minutes)
1. Draw Sphero
2. Write: "To control Sphero, I..."
3. Draw the path you drove
4. Rate: Easy üòä Medium üòê Hard üò∞

### Closing Circle (2 minutes)
1. **What We Learned** ‚Äî "Who's in charge‚Äîus or Sphero?"
2. **Responsible Tech** ‚Äî "How do we use technology well?"
3. **Closing Prayer** ‚Äî *"Thank you, God, for robots and technology. Help us use them to do good things. Amen."*

## Differentiation

### For Students Who Need Support

- Extra time with controls

- Simpler path (straight line)

- Partner driving

### For Advanced Students

- Complex path with turns

- Speed challenge (slow and accurate)

- Explore additional app features
