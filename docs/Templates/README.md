## Available Templates

### [Lesson Plan Template](./Lesson_Plan_Template.md)
Comprehensive template for individual C-STEM lessons. Includes sections for:

- Faith-Reason Integration

- Hands-on activities

- 21st Century skills development

- Catholic heritage connections

- Service orientation

- Parent resources

**Best for:** Single class period lessons (30-60 minutes)

### [Project-Based Learning Template](./Project_Based_Learning_Template.md)

- "Hands and Feet of Christ" service component

- Catholic Social Teaching alignment

- Community connection opportunities

**Best for:** Extended projects (1-4 weeks)

---

### [Cross-Curricular Unit Template](./Cross_Curricular_Unit_Template.md)
Template for units that integrate STEM with other subjects. Includes:

- STREAM integration map

- Subject-by-subject faith connections

- Weekly planning grids

- Collaboration planning for multiple teachers

**Best for:** Multi-subject integrated units

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose the appropriate template** based on your lesson/project duration
2. **Complete all sections** to ensure comprehensive faith integration
3. **Use the checklists** at the end of each template to verify success factor alignment
4. **Reference the rubrics** to understand how lessons will be evaluated
