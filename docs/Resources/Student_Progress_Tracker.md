## üí° Tips for Tracking

1. **Keep it simple** ‚Äî You don't need to track everything every time
2. **Use sticky notes** ‚Äî Jot observations during class, transfer later
3. **Focus on growth** ‚Äî Compare students to themselves, not others
4. **Include student voice** ‚Äî Use their reflections as data
5. **Celebrate progress** ‚Äî Share growth with students and parents
